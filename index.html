Welcome to StudentApp

StudentApp (also known as StudentHelper) is a desktop companion for university students. It integrates with Google Classroom to display coursework and assignments, fetches class schedules directly from the DUIKT university system, and manages online meetings through Zoom and Google Meet. The app provides offline access to your schedule and tasks, and supports multiple languages (Ukrainian and English).

Built using Python and PyQt5, StudentApp helps students organize their academic life by combining all course, schedule, and meeting data in one intuitive interface.
